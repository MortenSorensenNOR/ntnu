    \item Vi lar $F = \frac{R1}{R2} = \frac{R0}{R3}$. F책r da

            E_{out} &= E_0 \left(\frac{1}{\frac{R(T)}{R_3} + 1} - \frac{1}{\frac{R_1}{R_2} + 1} \right) \\
                    &= E_0 \left(\frac{1}{F\left(1 + \frac{\alpha \Delta T}{R_0}\right) + 1} - \frac{1}{F + 1} \right) \\
                    &= E_0 \left(\frac{1}{(F + 1) + F\frac{\alpha \Delta T}{R_0}} - \frac{1}{F + 1} \right) \\
                    &= E_0 \left(\frac{1}{(F + 1)\left(1 + \frac{F}{F+1}\frac{\alpha \Delta T}{R_0}\right)} - \frac{1}{F + 1} \right) \\
                    &\approx E_0 \left(\frac{1}{F + 1}\left(1 - \frac{F}{F+1}\frac{\alpha \Delta T}{R_0}\right) - \frac{1}{F + 1} \right) \\
                    &= -E_0 \frac{F}{(F+1)^2}\frac{\alpha \Delta T}{R_0}
